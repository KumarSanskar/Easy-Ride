About EasyRide

EasyRide one of the world's largest ride-hailing companies, serving 250+ cities across India, Australia, New Zealand, and the UK. ... EasyRide was founded in Dec 1998 by Narayan Oberai with a mission to build mobility for a billion people. The EasyRide offers mobility solutions by connecting customers to drivers and a wide range of vehicles across bikes, auto-rickshaws, metered taxis, and cabs, enabling convenience and transparency for hundreds of millions of consumers and over 1.5 million driver-partners. EasyRide’s core mobility offering in India is supplemented by its electric-vehicle arm,EasyRide Electric; India’s largest fleet management business, EasyRide Fleet Technologies and EasyRide Skilling, that aims to enable millions of livelihood opportunities for India's youth.
